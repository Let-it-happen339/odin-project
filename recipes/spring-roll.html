Spring roll recipes.

Ingredients to make spring-roll.

  • Rice vermicelli.
  • Extra firm tofu.
  • Carrot and cucumber.

steps to making spring rolls.

  1. Assemble your ingredients together.
  2. Chop all of your vegetables into thin, long, slices.
  3. Dip your wrappers in water. (An inch of water on a plate is good)
  4. Add your filling.
  5. Now wrap.
  6. Thats it! Now eat it or serve it to your musty sister!
