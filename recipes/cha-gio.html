Cha gio

Cha gio is a traditonal food from Vietnam, my mother buys them frozen.

Ingredients for Cha gio

  • rice
  • sheets
  • ground pork
  • carrots
  • garlic

steps to making cha gio

  1. cook your ingredients in a pan.
  2. season with salt, pepper, and garlic.
  3. put your ingredients into the rice paper.
  4. roll your rice paper to form a nugget
  5. now fry your cha gio or bake them
